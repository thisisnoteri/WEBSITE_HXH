Manipulators are Nen users who specialize in controlling or influencing objects, people, or living beings using their aura. Their abilities allow them to manipulate movement, behavior, or actions, often through specific conditions like physical contact, commands, or signals. Manipulators excel at precision and control, making them strategic and tactical fighters.


What does a Manipulator do?

Manipulators use their aura to control or command targets, either directly or indirectly. This can range from controlling physical objects like weapons to influencing people or creatures. Their Nen abilities often involve clear rules or triggers that define how their manipulation works. Because of their focus on control, they tend to plan their moves carefully and exploit openings in battle.


Characteristics of Manipulator:

  • They excel at controlling objects, people, or movements with accuracy.
  • Their abilities tend to be flexible and imaginative, fitting their personal fighting style
  • Manipulators are usually logical, assertive, and detail-oriented, preferring calculated strategies over raw power.
