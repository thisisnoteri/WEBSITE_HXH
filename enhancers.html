In Hunter x Hunter (HxH), an Enhancer is one of the six main categories of Nen users based on their Nen abilities. Nen is the technique that allows a person to use and manipulate their own life energy (aura).


What does an Enhancer do?

Enhancers specialize in strengthening and reinforcing their own body or objects. They are known for their straightforward and powerful abilities that boost their physical capabilities. This makes them excellent in direct combat, often relying on raw power, enhanced speed, or durability.


Characteristics of Enhancers:

  • They excel in close combat because their abilities often directly strengthen their body or attacks.
  • Their abilities tend to be simple but powerful.
  • Enhancers usually have a strong willpower and are straightforward in nature.
