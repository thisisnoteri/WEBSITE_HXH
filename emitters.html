Emitters are Nen users who specialize in releasing or projecting their aura away from their bodies. This allows them to create abilities that involve shooting energy blasts, controlling aura at a distance, or generating explosive force. Their power focuses on speed, range, and impact, making them highly effective in offensive and mid-to-long-range combat.


What does an Emitter do?

Emitters specialize in projecting their aura away from their body, allowing them to create powerful long-range attacks or release energy blasts. Their abilities often involve speed, impact, and precision at a distance, making them excellent for offensive and ranged combat. Emitters can also manipulate aura that has been separated from their body for a short time, though maintaining control over it becomes harder the farther it travels.


Characteristics of Emitters:

  • They excel in ranged or projectile-based combat, where aura is shot or emitted outward.
  • Their abilities often involve explosions, beams, or rapid aura bursts that emphasize power and speed.
  • Emitters are usually energetic, impulsive, and action-oriented individuals who prefer direct and decisive approaches.
