Nen Types

In Hunter × Hunter, Nen (念) is a fundamental concept, representing the ability to manipulate one’s own life energy, called aura, to perform extraordinary feats. Every Nen user has a specific Nen type, which defines how they naturally control and utilize their aura. There are six main Nen types: Enhancement, Emission, Manipulation, Transmutation, Conjuration, and Specialization.

Each type reflects a unique way of shaping aura and is linked to certain personality traits and abilities. This system adds depth to the combat and strategy in Hunter × Hunter, as characters develop complex and personalized Nen abilities based on their Nen type and creativity.
